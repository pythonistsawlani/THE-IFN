# IFN Platform - Quick Start Guide

## 🚀 Getting Started

Welcome to the IFN (ICFAI Founders Network) platform! This guide will help you navigate and understand the complete application.

## 📱 Application Overview

IFN is a comprehensive incubator management system with **20+ interconnected pages** serving startups, founders, mentors, and administrators.

## 🗺️ Navigation Guide

### Main Navigation (Sidebar)

**Primary Menu:**
1. **Dashboard** (`/dashboard`) - Your home base with stats and activity
2. **Feed** (`/feed`) - Browse and create posts
3. **Calendar** (`/calendar`) - View events and workshops
4. **Idea Pipeline** (`/pipeline`) - Track ideas through 6 gates
5. **Problems Hub** (`/problems`) - Find problems to solve
6. **Team Board** (`/team-board`) - Find co-founders and team members
7. **Autopsy Library** (`/autopsy`) - Learn from failures
8. **Admin Panel** (`/admin`) - Platform management (Admins only)
9. **Settings** (`/settings`) - Your account settings

### Top Navigation (Header)

- **Search Bar** - Global search across all content
- **Notifications** 🔔 - Real-time activity updates (red dot = new)
- **Profile** 👤 - Quick access to your profile

## 🎯 Key Features Walkthrough

### 1. First Time Login

**Start here:** `/login`

1. Enter credentials or register
2. You'll land on **Dashboard**
3. Explore the quick stats and recent activity
4. Click "Create New Post" or use sidebar to navigate

### 2. Browse Content

**Go to:** `/feed`

- View all posts in the platform
- Switch tabs: **All Posts** | **Trending** | **Following**
- Click any post to see details
- **Like** ❤️ **Comment** 💬 **Share** 🔗 **Bookmark** 🔖

### 3. Create a Post

**Go to:** `/posts/create` or click "Create Post" button

1. Enter compelling title
2. Write detailed description
3. Select relevant tags (EdTech, AI, HealthTech, etc.)
4. Choose: **Save as Draft** or **Publish Post**

### 4. Submit an Idea to Pipeline

**Go to:** `/pipeline` → Click "Submit Idea"

Fill out the submission form:
- **Idea Title** - Clear and catchy
- **Problem Statement** - What problem does it solve?
- **Proposed Solution** - Your approach
- **Target Market** - Who are your customers?
- **Categories** - Select relevant tags
- **Team Size** - How many members?

Your idea will enter the **6-gate validation process:**
1. ⭕ Ideation
2. ⭕ Validation
3. ⭕ Prototyping
4. ⭕ Testing
5. ⭕ Launch Ready
6. ⭕ Launched

### 5. Track Your Idea

**Go to:** `/pipeline/:id` (click on your idea)

View:
- Current gate and progress
- Milestones and checkpoints
- Team members
- Gate requirements
- Advance to next gate when ready

### 6. Explore Problems

**Go to:** `/problems`

- Browse real-world problems
- **Upvote** 👍 problems you find interesting
- **Comment** with insights or questions
- Filter by category
- Post your own problem

### 7. Find Team Members

**Go to:** `/team-board`

- Browse openings by role:
  - Developers
  - Designers
  - Business/Marketing
- Filter by skills needed
- View location (Remote/On-site)
- **Apply** to join teams

### 8. Learn from Failures

**Go to:** `/autopsy`

Each autopsy includes:
- **Why it failed** - Root causes
- **Key lessons** - What to avoid
- **Investment & Duration** - Resources spent
- **Category** - Industry type

Filter by category and learn from others' mistakes!

### 9. Manage Events

**Go to:** `/calendar`

- View month calendar
- See upcoming events:
  - Workshops
  - Mentor sessions
  - Demo days
  - Networking events
- **Register** for events
- View event details

### 10. Customize Your Profile

**Go to:** `/profile`

View your:
- **Posts** - All your published posts
- **Ideas** - Ideas in pipeline
- **Activity** - Recent actions

**Edit Profile** to update:
- Name and bio
- Location and website
- Interest tags
- Profile picture

### 11. Search Everything

**Go to:** `/search` or use header search bar

Search across:
- 📝 Posts
- 💡 Ideas
- 👥 People
- ❓ Problems

Use tabs to filter results or view all at once.

### 12. Check Notifications

**Go to:** `/notifications` or click 🔔 in header

Notification types:
- ❤️ **Likes** - Someone liked your content
- 💬 **Comments** - New comments on your posts
- 👥 **Team** - Team applications or acceptances
- 🚀 **Pipeline** - Your idea advanced gates
- 📢 **Mentions** - Someone mentioned you

**Mark as read** to clear notifications.

### 13. Adjust Settings

**Go to:** `/settings`

**Account Information:**
- Update name, email, bio
- Change password
- Update location

**Notification Preferences:**
- Toggle email notifications
- Control which activities notify you

**Privacy Settings:**
- Profile visibility
- Email display preferences

## 👨‍💼 Admin Features

*Available only to administrators*

### Admin Dashboard

**Go to:** `/admin`

- Pending reviews

**Quick Actions:**
- Manage Tags
- Pipeline Management
- User Management
- Content Moderation

### Manage Tags

**Go to:** `/admin/tags`

- View all tags and usage counts
- **Add** new tags
- **Edit** existing tags
- **Delete** unused tags

### Pipeline Management

**Go to:** `/admin/pipeline`

Review pending idea submissions:
- View submission details
- **Approve** ✅ to accept into pipeline
- **Reject** ❌ with feedback

## 🆘 Getting Help

### Common Questions

**Q: How do I create a post?**
A: Click "Create Post" button in header or sidebar, or navigate to `/posts/create`

**Q: Where do I submit my idea?**
A: Go to Pipeline (`/pipeline`) and click "Submit Idea" button

**Q: How do I join a team?**
A: Browse Team Board (`/team-board`) and click "Apply" on openings

**Q: Can I save drafts?**
A: Yes! When creating posts, click "Save as Draft" button

**Q: How do I see my notifications?**
A: Click the bell icon 🔔 in the header or go to `/notifications`
